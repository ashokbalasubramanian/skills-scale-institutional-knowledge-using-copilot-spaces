# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Additional Personas

### UX Designer

**Role Summary**
Shapes the user experience, conducts research, creates mockups, and collaborates on usability goals throughout the design and delivery process.

**Responsibilities**
- Research user needs and validate usability
- Design wireframes and prototypes
- Ensure product features align with UX standards
- Review acceptance criteria for design implications
- Collaborate with developers and QA during implementation

**Typical Interactions**
- Works closely with Product Managers to define customer journeys
- Partners with Developers to resolve design challenges
- Advises QA on usability-related test cases

---

### Scrum Master / Agile Coach

**Role Summary**
Facilitates team ceremonies, helps remove obstacles, and drives continuous improvement and healthy team dynamics.

**Responsibilities**
- Facilitate standups, retrospectives, and sprint planning
- Advocate for agile best practices
- Support team health and unblock progress
- Coach new members in agile workflows

**Typical Interactions**
- Partners with PMs and Developers for flow optimization
- Escalates impediments as needed
- Coordinates with QA and Support for sprint outcomes

---

### Tech Lead

**Role Summary**
Guides technical direction, architecture choices, and aligns solutions with roadmap and business goals.

**Responsibilities**
- Own technical design and solution feasibility
- Mentor developers and review code/architecture
- Assess technical risks and mitigate them
- Align technical plans with product vision

**Typical Interactions**
- Collaborates with PM/PdM on scoping and prioritization
- Guides Developers during design and implementation phases

---

### Release Manager

**Role Summary**
Orchestrates release planning and readiness, ensuring smooth deployments and timely stakeholder communication.

**Responsibilities**
- Plan release timelines and activities
- Prepare and verify deployment checklists
- Coordinate with QA and Support for release verification
- Announce releases and track post-release issues

**Typical Interactions**
- Works with Developers for release handover
- Interfaceswith QA for verification
- Collaborates with PM for scheduling and with Support for post-release follow-up

---

### Support Lead

**Role Summary**
Manages post-release support, monitors customer issues, and leads the incident response and improvement cycles.

**Responsibilities**
- Monitor post-release metrics and support channels
- Coordinate incident response and triage customer-reported issues
- Maintain support documentation
- Drive postmortem reviews and follow-up actions

**Typical Interactions**
- Collaborates with Release Manager on launches and triage
- Works with Developers for bug diagnosis
- Partners with Product Managers for feature feedback

---

- Use these persona definitions to frame scenarios and sample interactions in the Skills Exercise.
